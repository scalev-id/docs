---
 You can add multiple business members to your Scalev account.

The number of business members depends on the plan you're on.

For business member roles, Scalev provides 9 roles:

1. Admin
    

3. Warehouse Admin
    

5. Accounting Staff
    

7. Inventory Manager
    

9. Sales Person
    

11. Advertiser
     

13. Store Manager
     

15. Payment Staff
     

17. Viewer All
     
       
     

For the Privileges of each member role, see here: [https://tutorial.scalev.id/role](https://tutorial.scalev.id/role)

* * *

{/* more */}

Here's the tutorial for adding members to your Scalev account:

#1. Go to the Scalev dashboard, then click the **Setting** menu

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Screenshot-at-Feb-20-11-02-31.png)

⇣

#2. Then click the **Members** sub-menu and click **+Add Member** 

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/CleanShot-2024-02-20-at-11.06.55.png)

⇣

#3. Enter the member's email address.

#4. Enter the member's phone number

#5. Choose the member's role.

#6. Click **Save**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/CleanShot-2024-02-20-at-13.48.41.png)

After that, the added member will receive an invitation email like this 👇

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/CleanShot-2024-02-20-at-13.53.20.png)

Your new business member can directly click the button: **Verify Email**

Or click the link below it.

⇣

They'll then be redirected to the new Scalev signup page like this 👇

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/CleanShot-2024-02-20-at-13.54.21.png)

Then fill in:

1. Full name

3. Password.

5. Check **Agree to the Applicable Terms & Conditions**

7. Click the **Sign Up Now** button

You'll then be redirected to the Scalev login page.

Enter the email & password you just created.

Then click: **Sign In Now**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/CleanShot-2024-02-20-at-14.01.12.png)

If successful, your new business member account will be redirected to your Scalev dashboard.

This signals that the member-add process on your Scalev account has **succeeded.**
