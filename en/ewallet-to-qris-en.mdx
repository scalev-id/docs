If you didn't enable QRIS payment but a customer ordered using QRIS, you may have enabled one or more of these eWallets:

1. GoPay

3. DANA

5. LinkAja

These three eWallets automatically convert to QRIS when your customer **fills out the order form** directly from Desktop (PC/tablet) **not from mobile.**

This is because these three eWallets don't provide a dedicated QRIS that can be opened directly from desktop.

* * *

##### **Questions:**

**Can the customer still pay for the order?**

Yes, the customer can still pay for the order — just using QRIS. 😇

**Does the money still go into E-Payment Balance?**

Don't worry, the money your customer pays still goes into your E-Payment Balance. 👌

**Does the transaction fee follow the original payment method or QRIS?**

It follows QRIS 🙏
