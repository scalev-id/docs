Check speed here: [https://pagespeed.web.dev](https://pagespeed.web.dev/)

Why does the score keep changing?

1\. The score is checked through Google's random network. Sometimes their network isn't "friendly" with Indonesian ISPs. But generally it's fine.

2\. The largest components loaded by a website are usually 2: pixel and image.

Often Meta pixel is unstable, or stalls midway. If that's the case, only God knows. To check, sometimes pagespeed shows something like this:

![Faq Pagespeed Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/faq-pagespeed-min.png)

This case means the pixel stalled or failed to execute.

If the case is the image, just follow the tutorial to treat the image.

How do we know Scalev's actual performance without pixel issues interfering?

1. Duplicate your landing page.

3. Remove all installed pixels including their events.

5. Save.

7. Test with pagespeed.

You'll find the actual performance result of the Scalev landing page.
