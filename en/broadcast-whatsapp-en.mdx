WhatsApp broadcast via Waylev is:

- ✅ _\*_90% safe

- ✅ Fast (no delay needed)

- 🥲 Costs Rp 600 per recipient (tax included)

* * *

### 1\. Click the "Ads & WhatsApp" menu

![Click the 'Ads & WhatsApp' menu](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2FZqEW26cryVcB9j38KZUcasYRXdw1%2FrJ1XD1Ya7tNLzxtAVyFWLd%2F8HEqEPFfNnuTMGBY3Dbd2e_doc.png?alt=media&token=dec3a5dc-4ab8-41f9-a7dd-7a09123cfa49)

### 2\. Click the "Send WhatsApp Message" sub-menu

![Click the 'Send WhatsApp Message' sub-menu](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2FZqEW26cryVcB9j38KZUcasYRXdw1%2FrJ1XD1Ya7tNLzxtAVyFWLd%2Fo2wy4jvDTVbr3pzgf25Txb_doc.png?alt=media&token=f0062996-3fba-4d81-8825-96b01f3603dd)

### 3\. Click the "Send Message" button

![Click the 'Send Message' button](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2FZqEW26cryVcB9j38KZUcasYRXdw1%2FrJ1XD1Ya7tNLzxtAVyFWLd%2FwALZPzTwhLWP2BD5YtK8Jp_doc.png?alt=media&token=bcf58844-dc03-46df-8dcb-8ffc84a6a1bb)

### 4\. Click "Select Message Template" to pick the template you want to broadcast

To create a message template you want to broadcast, you have to create it first in your Business Manager account.

Through here: [https://business.facebook.com/latest/whatsapp\_manager/message\_templates](https://business.facebook.com/latest/whatsapp_manager/message_templates)

The process is:

1. You create the message template you want to broadcast
    
2. Then wait for approval from Meta
    

_\*Why 90% safe?_

1. Because what you want to broadcast goes through Meta's approval.
    
2. No matter how many recipients you have
    

In fact, Meta is happy when more people receive your broadcast (💰).

![Click 'Select Message Template' to pick the template you want to broadcast](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2FZqEW26cryVcB9j38KZUcasYRXdw1%2FrJ1XD1Ya7tNLzxtAVyFWLd%2F28ovZDU5BYrcLSD2nXD9tU_doc.png?alt=media&token=16fe9589-16c3-4d27-b01c-772b86cce4f6)

### 5\. Click "Choose File" to upload a CSV file containing:

1. NAME
    
2. WHATSAPP NUMBER of broadcast recipients
    

![Click 'Choose File' to upload a CSV file containing:](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2FZqEW26cryVcB9j38KZUcasYRXdw1%2FrJ1XD1Ya7tNLzxtAVyFWLd%2FjCrtaVHnNU2Rgm226zdJkJ_doc.png?alt=media&token=aa97c8cc-d99f-4ad8-b896-e982ac04565c)

### 6\. Click "Send"

- If you check "_Prevent Sending to Existing Customer_", customers who have ever contacted your WABA on Scalev Chat will not receive the broadcast (excluded from broadcast recipients).
    
- If you check "_Prevent Sending to Active Customer_", customers who have received your broadcast in the last 7 days will not receive your new broadcast (excluded from broadcast recipients).
    

⇣

# WARNING  
**before clicking** the "Send" button!

Once clicked, the broadcast process starts immediately!

Which means:

1. The broadcast is actually sent.
    
2. You will be charged for the delivery (depending on how many in your audience receive the broadcast).
    

![Click 'Send'](https://static.guidde.com/v0/qg%2FZqEW26cryVcB9j38KZUcasYRXdw1%2FrJ1XD1Ya7tNLzxtAVyFWLd%2FiDUCSnDapvPFnFAePaDmNp_doc.png?alt=media&token=ec7a0690-8d74-4880-8719-1d59934f447b)
