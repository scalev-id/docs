How to Integrate Woowa with Scalev

To integrate your Woowa account with your Scalev account, follow these steps:

1\. Go to the **Setting** menu.

2\. Click **Integrasi**.

3\. Select **Integrasi dengan WhatsApp Gateway**

4\. Click **Tambah Integrasi**  
  
![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/CleanShot-2024-03-25-at-07.29.40-1024x565.png)

5\. Select Woowa.

6\. Log in to your Woowa account.

7\. Click **More** in the **Action** column

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/1-1-1024x583.png)

8\. When the option appears, click **Get Key.**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/2-1-1024x584.png)

Wait until the Woowa API Key appears.

9\. Copy your License Code & Woowa Key

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/3-1-1024x577.png)

10\. Paste everything into Scalev.

11\. Click **Save**.

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/CleanShot-2024-03-25-at-07.32.26.png)
