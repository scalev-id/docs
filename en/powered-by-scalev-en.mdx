---
title: "How to Remove the \"Powered by Scalev\" Branding in LMS?"
description: "There's no special method or setting to remove or hide it."
---
There's no special method or setting to remove or hide it.

The only way is for your plan to be at least Basic.

Basic plan and above, the "Powered by Scalev" branding automatically disappears 😚

Don't want our branding tagging along? [Upgrade](https://scalev.id/plan)!
