![Cleanshot 2025 07 18 at 16.53.22@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-18-at-16.53.22@2x.png)

- Go to Accounts → WhatsApp Account in your Business Manager.

- Click the WhatsApp account you want to add a payment method to.

- Click the three dots on the right.

- Select Payment Setting.

* * *

![Cleanshot 2025 07 18 at 16.55.11@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-18-at-16.55.11@2x.png)

- When it appears, click Add payment method.

* * *

- If you've already linked your credit card to your Business Manager before, select Business Payment Method & click Next

![Cleanshot 2025 07 20 at 12.13.48@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-20-at-12.13.48@2x.png)

Then select the credit card to use & click Set as default

![Cleanshot 2025 07 20 at 12.08.22@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-20-at-12.08.22@2x.png)

⇣

If you haven't linked your credit card to your Business Manager yet, select Debit or credit card & click Next

![Cleanshot 2025 07 18 at 16.55.52@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-18-at-16.55.52@2x.png)

Enter your credit card details & click Save

![Cleanshot 2025 07 18 at 16.57.35@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-18-at-16.57.35@2x.png)

You'll then be asked to enter an OTP — complete the credit card registration and click Save as Default.

Scalev WABA is part of the Waylev (Scalev Chat) service.
