---
title: "How to Add ePayment at Scalev"
description: "A step-by-step guide to adding ePayment as a payment method in your Scalev store"
---

To add ePayment as a payment method to your store, follow the steps below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Stores Menu">
    From the Scalev dashboard, navigate to the **Stores** menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Store">
    Choose your store name from the list that appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Payment Method Settings">
    Scroll down to the **Payment Method** section, then click **Select Payment Method**.

    <img src="https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/tambah-epayment-1-1024x586.png" alt="Payment Method Page" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable ePayment">
    A dialog box will appear as shown below.

    <img src="https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/tambah-epayment-2.png" alt="Payment Method Dialog" />

    - Click the dropdown and select **Invoice**
    - Check the option **"Enable ALL E-Payment Methods"**
    - Click the **Save** button inside the dialog box
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Settings">
    To confirm that your settings have been saved, click the **Save** button located in the upper-right corner of the screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

At this point, you have successfully added ePayment as a payment method to your Scalev order form.
