Before you can advertise your Scalev Landing Page on Facebook, you must integrate the active ad account you'll use for advertising into Scalev.

The main function of this ad account integration is to let you collect customer data into Custom Audiences in Meta.

So your Custom Audience will always update automatically without any manual action from you after integration.

Follow these steps to integrate your ad account with Scalev.

1\. Make sure you're logged into Facebook first

2\. Log in to the Scalev dashboard then go to Settings → Integrasi → Click the Facebook tab → Click + Tambah Integrasi Facebook

![Integrasi Ad Account 1](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/integrasi-ad-account-1-1024x559.png)

3\. When the dialog appears, click "Continue with Facebook"

<figcaption>

</figure>

4\. Once your ad account is successfully integrated with Scalev, it looks like this:

<figure>

![Integrasi Ad Account 3](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/integrasi-ad-account-3-1024x751.png)

<figcaption>

At this point you've successfully integrated your ad account with Scalev.
