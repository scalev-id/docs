For incoming order notifications via Telegram, each business member must connect their Telegram account to the Scalev Bot.

Before starting, make sure you're logged into your Telegram account on the _Device_ you're using to open Scalev.

Then follow the steps below:

1. Open the **Setting** menu

3. Select **Profile**

5. Click **Connect Telegram account.**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/CleanShot-2024-03-21-at-10.14.40.png)

You'll be directed to open your Telegram ⇢ Scalev Bot.

Then click **Start**.

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/CleanShot-2024-03-21-at-09.58.32.png)

\---

If successful, a notification appears like this:

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/CleanShot-2024-03-21-at-10.07.03.png)

We recommend creating an order on one of your landing pages.

To verify incoming order notifications are received like this:

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/CleanShot-2024-03-21-at-10.10.32.png)
