Order notifications not arriving in Telegram even though you've integrated and linked your TG account? Here's how to fix it:

## Re-integrate

1. **Delete your Telegram Account.**

Go to Settings > Profile > Delete Telegram Account

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjryOXB5CG0-Winoxz_q4xyR3izoyOA_mTnVyNQHaTjuEvhh_TVdDXEoX6xzp1WymFA47iLCXIgCor6OwiLY2AKbZm0TnPpLqeLxPwq12E33tAGOPnh4D-ePfygD71TayC_dfhLlmBHqTfNR2fTzjiYRgE0J3xP3VI809_28ruLaUnmAaV3PAum9V-LUgQ/s1600/Z.webp)

2\. **Reconnect Your Telegram Account**

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjZZKosx_Erq9gJp_eqfAYDK13sBVAeDgQJLXFJljRS1lwqeern-3kdysZ25dKFcRq1C6CBlyXNwbKeJmpbb7y3HJmh0YfWbglfVSH4HX0I59a4FDKhi_qHHUsfYsq0di5Tu9eCyOlZEF72kYr79RMc_SmVv_J-bwGTnoXvI5XFO9mWwfwNnvqYl400cSA/s1600/Z1.webp)

## Uncheck Notifications

Still in Settings > Profile, click **Configure Order Notification with Telegram**

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEh8QyPFXcl2Q_afEoN2MVCpSnquwlOf1_H63i-m97M3kHGU0qBH-U6W3pzYNCZkMmmbdAr3gEpLtORrKE8UxhL8uavfEFpZ3gGm2nIjSCFbC0kZwLNzGZczNm6bFi4eCBQqBz9ixmTjWRg7jSCWn7WMJdiheIRMbSVHhS4Q6AUBQGepwzlce2mDO_CpEio/s1600/Z2.webp)

Uncheck **all items** in the notification configuration. Then click **save**.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEihtqzrmJ13OV970B_g1SbsGFT3Zfl2k2WDpZV3MCthBfyxUXaQrmd0_lYj9Rme0EsoUpdpAYoFn1CN48LGB0GUItyfjFbkpvo0xryEoWd41G2pa9IybJNZm7BXhPlLIhPbuIvPDI6eZV6kMjvkzGOt_wAaYbM9HsjaD34tHgJaLjZqEXZUnTG3rlfltHk/s1600/Z3.png)

## Test Order 1 (REQUIRED)

After unchecking the notification settings, go to your landing page and place a test order with complete details. This test order is required.

Note: No notifications will be sent to Telegram for this first test order. Continue.

## Check Notifications

After the test order, go back to **Configure Order Notification with Telegram** and check all items again. Then click **save**.

![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhGxGnwUzGLgEpeiO3M3anV95_fdp5JJypEJJ-MDEr_AOs3PZ0Bltxn3TsSoPu8_kQdH6xqWX0VQAUgvjx50mW7S1ryH55heT6GqXTisAinhTTq90mSwaIJGZds2phZ7P0_mXD8JAgRB0oQXNoxIQdItfw04UUr80S3AAX13ullyQFH_lTk9VAwGEdNNug/s1600/Z5.png)

## Done

Place another test order. Check for the notification in Telegram.

## **Important:** Follow the steps above in order.
