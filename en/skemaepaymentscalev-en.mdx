This article discusses **Scalev fee**.

That is: the fee Scalev charges on orders paid using e-payment.

Payment methods classified as e-payment include:

- QRIS,

- VA (virtual account),

- Gopay,

- LinkAja,

- Dana,

- Ovo,

- Shopeepay,

.

Bank transfer payment method in Scalev has zero fees!

**Closing #1:**

The fee in the video above refers to the fee charged by Scalev (Scalev fee).

The fee charged by the payment gateway is separate. That's called "Transaction Fee".

See here: [https://scalev.id/transaction-fee](https://scalev.id/transaction-fee)

.

_\*Any platform_ **_will_** _charge transaction fees to its users. Scalev is no exception._
