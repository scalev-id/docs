Scalev now has a feature to view Meta ad data directly from your Scalev dashboard.

It's called **Ad Funnel**.

Previously, you had to open the Meta ad dashboard on a laptop to see ad statistics. Now you can just open your Scalev dashboard.

Accessible anytime you want, from a laptop or phone.

Convenient!

**REQUIREMENTS**

Before creating an Ad Funnel, make sure your Scalev account is integrated with Facebook.

If not, follow the Facebook integration guide here: [https://tutorial.scalev.id/integrasi-ad-account/](https://tutorial.scalev.id/integrasi-ad-account/)

Follow this tutorial to create an Ad Funnel that you can fully customize to your needs.

1. Click the **Ads & WhatsApp** menu

3. Click the **Configuration** menu

5. Click the **Create Ad Funnel** button

<figure>

![Ad funnel 1](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/ad-funnel-1.png)

<figcaption>

Steps to create a new Ad Funnel

</figcaption>

</figure>

Then on the **Create Ad Funnel** page, fill in the available fields.

<figure>

![Ad funnel 2](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/ad-funnel-2.png)

<figcaption>

Ad Funnel creation process

</figcaption>

</figure>

1. **Ad Funnel Name**: enter whatever name you want, e.g.: Custom View 1

3. **Ad Funnel applies to**: select the ad account connected to your Scalev account.

5. **Funnel level 1**: select the first event in your funnel — typically Landing Page View (PageView) or Content Views (ViewContent).

7. **Funnel level 2**: select the next event after Landing Page View — typically when a landing page with a checkout form is opened. Example: Adds to Cart.

9. **Funnel level 3**: select the next event after Adds to Cart — typically when the checkout form is submitted. Example: Checkouts Initiated.

11. **Funnel level 4**: select the final event after Checkouts Initiated — this is your goal, Purchase.  
       
     Check **Make this level as a goals** to set this event as the goal of your funnel.

Then click Save to save this new Ad Funnel.

The Flow of Events above is automatically pre-filled with Scalev's default selection, with Purchase as the goal when you first create it.

After creating the funnel, check and view the results in the **Ads & WhatsApp** menu.

Select the Ad Funnel name you created earlier from the filter below the **Advertising** heading.

If Purchase isn't the final goal of your funnel, you can customize it however you like.

Set each funnel level yourself with the event stages that match your funnel.

Click the red X icon to remove a funnel level.

<figure>

![Custom view 5](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/custom-view-5.png)

<figcaption>

</figcaption>

</figure>

Then click **Add funnel level** to add a new funnel level.
