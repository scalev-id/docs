Then you can configure the following:

![Cleanshot 2025 06 07 at 12.16.19@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/CleanShot-2025-06-07-at-12.16.19@2x-700x1024.png)

To set up CS rotation, go back to the **STORE** menu, then find the **SALES PERSON** sub-menu

![Cleanshot 2025 06 07 at 12.18.57@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/CleanShot-2025-06-07-at-12.18.57@2x-1024x584.png)

Then configure who receives incoming leads in your WhatsApp Business here:

![Cleanshot 2025 06 07 at 12.20.19@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/CleanShot-2025-06-07-at-12.20.19@2x.png)

Note that the sales person list shown here only includes people **already added** as members of your business at the sales person level and above (including you, if you're the owner). If not added yet, no other people will appear here.
