title: "Why Can't I Add a New Business?"
description: "If you try to create a new business in the Business menu."
---
## Problem:

If you try to create a new business in the Business menu.

![Screen Shot 2024 12 24 At 14.19.47](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Screen-Shot-2024-12-24-at-14.19.47.png)

And after filling in your new business data, this notification appears:

![Cleanshot 2024 12 24 At 14.18.35](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/CleanShot-2024-12-24-at-14.18.35.png)

It means your free-plan Scalev business account has hit the limit.

⇣

Scalev limits 1 user to only 1 free-plan Scalev business account.

## **Example #1:**

You have 1 Scalev business account on the Free plan.

In this case you can't add another business.

## Solution:

- Upgrade your business account plan from Free to at least Lite first.

- Then you can create a new business account.

## Example #2:

You already have 2 Scalev business accounts:

- 1 using Lite/Basic/Pro/Ultimate plan.

- 1 using the Free plan.

In this case you also can't add another business.

## Solution:

- Upgrade the Free plan business account to at least Lite first.

- Then you can create a new business account.
