What if you want to use a landing page in a different Scalev account?

# For example:

- A business owner who wants to share their landing page as a reference for employees/advertiser friends. Sharing with fellow internet marketers. Or giving a reference landing page to their students. Or using it as an affiliate bonus.

- A product owner whose landing page will be advertised by affiliates, resellers, dropshippers.

- A landing page service provider who wants to quickly serve many clients by building landing pages efficiently.

Not possible before!

⇣ 

# Good News:

Now you can.

With the landing page export-import feature.

1. A landing page can be exported. The export result is a txt file.

3. The txt file can be imported into another Scalev account. 

5. Identical landing page, no differences.

# Tutorial:

**1/ Export Landing Page:**

1. On the landing page you want to export, there's a three-dot menu on the far right,

3. Click the three dots,

5. Select "Export". Save & name the file.

![Published Titik Tiga](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/published-titik-tiga.png)

​.

![Export Lp](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/export-lp.png)

\===

**2/ Import Landing Page:**

1. Go to the "Landing Page" menu

3. Click the "+ Add Landing Page" button

5. Select "Import". Choose the landing page file to import & Upload

​

![Tambah Landing Page](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/tambah-landing-page.png)

.

![Import Dari File](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/import-dari-file.png)
