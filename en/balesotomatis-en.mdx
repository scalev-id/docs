BalesOtomatis.id is a platform that provides a range of features to optimize marketing and customer follow-up services through WhatsApp.

Its main features:

- Smart Auto-Reply with AI

- Unlimited Broadcast

- Follow-Up Series

- Accurate Shipping Cost Check

- Magic Form Builder

- Conversion Tracking

BalesOtomatis also integrates with various systems and provides an API for further customization.

This includes webhook integration, which Scalev also supports.

The integration here is limited to webhooks only.

It is not yet a _deep integration_ like [Starsender](https://tutorial.scalev.id/starsender/ "How to Integrate Starsender with Scalev (For Auto Follow-Up by WhatsApp CS When Orders Come In)") or [Woowa](https://tutorial.scalev.id/woowa/ "How to Integrate Woowa with Scalev (For Auto Follow-Up by WhatsApp CS When Orders Come In)"), which already have built-in settings inside Scalev.

With this webhook, you can connect Scalev to BalesOtomatis.

This way you can configure WhatsApp follow-up templates exactly as you would inside Scalev.

But the configuration lives inside BalesOtomatis.

To integrate Scalev with BalesOtomatis, follow the tutorial on the official website via the link below:

[https://docs.balesotomatis.id/integrations/cara-integrasi-scalev-pakai-webhook](https://docs.balesotomatis.id/integrations/cara-integrasi-scalev-pakai-webhook)
