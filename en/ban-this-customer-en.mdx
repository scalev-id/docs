#### **The Function of This Feature:**

1. So a banned customer can no longer fill in the order form with the same data or use the same IP Address, minimizing spam orders from the same customer.  
    

3. In addition, banned customers will no longer be able to access your member area.

- Go to the "Order" menu → click the Order ID of the customer you want to ban.

![CleanShot 2025 11 11 at 10.10.03@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/CleanShot-2025-11-11-at-10.10.03@2x-1024x567.png)

- Scroll down to the "Customer Information" section → Click "Ban This Customer"

![CleanShot 2025 11 11 at 10.13.23@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/CleanShot-2025-11-11-at-10.13.23@2x-1024x607.png)

- A pop-up will appear like this; click "Save"

![CleanShot 2025 11 11 at 10.15.53@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/CleanShot-2025-11-11-at-10.15.53@2x-1.png)

* * *

A successful customer ban is signaled by 3 things:

1. The "Ban this customer" button disappears

3. A "Blocked" label appears on Customer Information

5. A "Suppressed" label appears on the customer's IP.

![CleanShot 2025 11 11 at 10.19.20@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/CleanShot-2025-11-11-at-10.19.20@2x-1024x964.png)
