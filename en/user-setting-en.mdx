After registering in Scalev, you can update your business user settings if they don't match your business needs.

Things you can change in business user settings:

1. Business avatar (not visible to your customers).

3. Business name (not visible to your customers).

5. Username (this affects the default slug you use in Scalev).

To change business user details, follow the steps below 👇

**#1.** Go to the **Setting** menu

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Screenshot-at-Feb-20-11-02-31.png)

**#2.** Click **Edit** in the Business sub-menu next to My Business

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Screenshot-at-Feb-20-11-04-09-1024x581.png)

**#3**. Upload Avatar (square size)

**#4.** Enter the business name you want.

**#5.** Enter the username (no spaces) to use as your Scalev slug.

**#6.** Click **Save**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Screenshot-at-Feb-20-11-06-06.png)

After that, My Business details in your Scalev sub-menu will update to match the new data you just entered.
