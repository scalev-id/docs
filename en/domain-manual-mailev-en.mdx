---
title: "How to Add a Domain to Mailev That Isn't Connected to Scalev"
description: "But now you can add your own custom domain even if it's not in Scalev's verified domain list."
---
Originally, the only custom domains that could be used with [Mailev](https://tutorial.scalev.id/category/mailev/ "Mailev") were domains already connected and added in Scalev.

But now you can add your own custom domain even if it's not in Scalev's verified domain list.

Follow these steps:

Log in to your Scalev dashboard

In your Scalev dashboard, click the Mailev menu icon on the left

<figure>

![Mailev identity manual](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/mailev-identity-manual-1-768x1024.png)

<figcaption>

Click the Mailev icon

</figcaption>

</figure>

You'll be redirected and logged in automatically to Mailev.

In the Mailev dashboard, click the dropdown menu then click **Identity**

<figure>

![Mailev identity manual](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/mailev-identity-manual-2-1024x576.png)

<figcaption>

Mailev dropdown menu

</figcaption>

</figure>

On the Identity page, click the **\+ Add Identity** button

<figure>

![Mailev identity manual](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/mailev-identity-manual-3-1024x825.png)

<figcaption>

Click the Identity menu

</figcaption>

</figure>

The **Add New Identity** dialog appears

<figure>

![Mailev identity manual](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/mailev-identity-manual-4-1024x400.png)

<figcaption>

Click the Add Identity button

</figcaption>

</figure>

Give this Identity a name in the **Title** field.

Then, click the **Enter manually** text to manually enter your custom domain.

<figure>

![Mailev identity manual](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/mailev-identity-manual-5.png)

<figcaption>

Click Enter Manually

</figcaption>

</figure>

Enter your domain without the http/https prefix in the **Domain** field.

Then follow the tutorial for adding an Identity in this article: [https://tutorial.scalev.id/mailev-identity/](https://tutorial.scalev.id/mailev-identity/)
