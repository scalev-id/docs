This feature lets other Scalev users sell the same product as a marketer through the following programs:

- Affiliator

- Affiliator Pro

- Dopfilliator

[See more here!](https://scalev.id/pilihan-collaboration)

Before enabling collaboration, make sure you've already created a product in the Product menu in Scalev.

Once that's done, go to the Product menu → Click the product name.

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 15.30.38@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-15.30.38@2x-1024x602.png)

* * *

Click "Open" in the "Collaboration" section on the right side of your product detail.

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 15.31.24@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-15.31.24@2x-1024x726.png)

* * *

You'll then be given 3 program options to enable

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 15.31.47@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-15.31.47@2x-1024x708.png)

If you're confused about the differences between the programs, watch the explanation video at [Explanation Video](https://youtu.be/XTH7qwburUs?si=K16VAfl-nPvcNNo7) or just click directly on the box provided.

* * *

#### Enabling the "Affiliate" program

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 15.32.49@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-15.32.49@2x-1003x1024.png)

##### **a. Fill in the Commission Rate percentage**

The percentage you enter here is the commission percentage your marketers in the Affiliator program will receive.

##### **b. Auto Approve anyone who requests**

When you enable this, anyone who requests to become your affiliator will be approved automatically. If not enabled, you can choose who to approve and who to reject.

##### **c. Invite New Affiliate Link.**

If you want to invite affiliators who don't yet have a Scalev account, you can give them this link.

* * *

#### Enabling the "Affiliate Pro" program

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 15.33.45@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-15.33.45@2x.png)

##### **a. Fill in the Commission Rate percentage**

The percentage you enter here is the commission percentage your marketers in the Affiliator Pro program will receive.

* * *

#### Enabling the "Dropfiliate" program

This program is only for Digital/LMS products

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 15.34.42@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-15.34.42@2x.png)

##### **a. Enter the Drofilliate price**

This is the price you'll receive when your marketer who is a Drofilliate successfully sells your product.

##### **b. Allow Drofilliate Domain for Product Page.**

When you enable this, your marketer who is a Drofilliate can sell your product using their own private domain.

* * *

#### General Collaboration Settings

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 15.36.32@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-15.36.32@2x-598x1024.png)

##### **a. Show in Collaboration**

If you enable this, your product will appear in the [Scalev collaboration menu](https://app.scalev.id/marketplace).

##### **b. Collaboration Invite Link.**

You can give this link to potential marketers so they can join your product's collaboration program without having to search through the collaboration menu.

##### **c. Collaboration Information Link**

If you have information related to your product's collaboration program, such as terms and conditions, you can build a landing page and put its link here.

So anyone who requests to be a marketer in your product's collaboration program can read it.

##### **d. Collaboration Kit Link**

If you have strategies, image assets, copywriting templates, and so on that can also be used by marketers in your product's collaboration program, you can put them on a landing page and put the landing page link here.

So anyone who is a marketer in your product's collaboration program can read and use them.

##### **e. Sales Letter for Collaboration**

You can fill this in with promotional content for your product targeted at potential marketers.

##### **f. Manage Marketers**

This is a shortcut to the [Marketer](https://app.scalev.id/partners/my-partners) menu in Scalev for managing your marketers.

Once everything is set, don't forget to click "**Save Change**".
