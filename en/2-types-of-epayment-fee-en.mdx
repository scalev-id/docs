## Prologue #1

The discussion on this page (scheme, terms, fee amounts, etc.) is OUTSIDE the "BANK TRANSFER" payment method.

For the bank transfer payment method on Scalev:

- FREE users and PAID users: AVAILABLE (allowed to use) ✅

- FREE users and PAID users: BUILT-IN (can use directly without any setup) ✅

- FREE users and PAID users: FREE (Scalev does not charge a single rupiah of fee on that order's invoice) ✅

⇣

## Prologue #2

There are 2 types of fees deducted when you use the E-Payment payment method

1. Payment Fee

3. Scalev Fee

⇣

## Discussion #1: Payment Fee

All Scalev plan tiers, whether...

- Free

- Lite

- Basic

- Pro

- Ultimate

All are subject to a payment Fee.

The fee amount depends on the type of E-payment.

You can see the amounts here → [https://scalev.id/transaction-fee](https://scalev.id/transaction-fee)

⇣

## Discussion #2: Scalev Fee

The Scalev Fee has 3 rates depending on conditions you have on your Scalev account:

#### **A. 5% Rate**

This rate applies under 2 conditions:

1. All plans still using the free subdomain (.scalev.id or .myscalev.com) on the checkout page link.

3. The Scalev Lite plan that has switched to a custom domain on the checkout page link.

\===

#### **B. 1% Rate**

This rate applies only to 3 specific Scalev plans:

1. Basic plan

3. Pro plan

5. Ultimate plan.

##### **With the requirement:**

Using a **custom domain (private domain / not the free subdomain (scalev.id / myscalev.com))** on the checkout page link.

Custom domain example: https://cart.kelaskonversi.com/kk-premium

\===
