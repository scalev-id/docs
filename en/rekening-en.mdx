---
title: "How to Add a Bank Account to Scalev"
description: "How to Add a Bank Account to Scalev"
---
Warning:

- Bank transfer payment method is only available on BASIC plan and above.

- Free & Lite plans cannot use the bank transfer payment method.

- Only the Owner can add a bank account

- Go to the **Setting** menu.

- Select **external payment**.

- Click **\+ Add Payment Method**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/CleanShot-2024-04-02-at-10.17.45-1024x575.png)

- Select **Bank Transfer**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/CleanShot-2024-04-03-at-05.13.13.png)

- Fill in your bank account details in the fields provided.

- If correct, click **Save.**

* * *

Notes:

1.  Bank Name

3. Bank Account Number

5. Account Holder Name

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/CleanShot-2024-04-03-at-05.14.23.png)

- Then open the **Store** menu.

- Select the store you want to add the account number to ⇢ Click Edit Store.

- Scroll down until you find **Payment Method.**

- Click **+Select Payment Method.**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/CleanShot-2024-04-03-at-05.32.08-1024x579.png)

- Check the bank account you just added. 

- Click Save.

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/CleanShot-2024-04-03-at-05.32.52.png)

- Finally, click **Save** in the top right corner of the Store settings**.**

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/CleanShot-2024-04-03-at-05.33.36-1024x582.png)
