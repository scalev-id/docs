Follow these steps to embed a Reels video from Instagram.

1. Open your IG profile  
    ![Embed reels ig 1](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/embed-reels-ig-1.jpeg)

3. Click the Reels tab to find the reels video you want to embed

5. Once the target video opens, click the three dots in the top right of the dialog

7. Then click the Embed menu  
    ![Embed reels ig 2](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/embed-reels-ig-2.png)

9. Click the **Copy embed code** button to copy the embed code

11. Check / uncheck to display the reels caption (optional)  
     ![Embed reels ig 3](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/embed-reels-ig-3.png)

13. Paste into the HTML component in Scalev Landing Page Studio (LPS)

15. Save & Publish to apply the changes
