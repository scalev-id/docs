Most likely, your Meta account or the phone number you used to register for WABA doesn't meet Meta's requirements.  
  
Check the main requirements you need to meet:

1\. Meta Business Verification

You must have a verified Facebook Business Manager ID. This verification process validates your business identity.

- **Legal Documents:** Prepare your business legal documents, such as:
    - Business License (SIUP/NIB)
    
    - Tax Registration Number (NPWP)
    
    - Company Deed of Establishment (PT, CV, or Foundation)

- **Business Information:** Meta will ask for details such as your official business name, address, and business phone number. 

2\. Digital Assets

- **Official Website:** Your business must have an active official website with its own domain.

- **Business Email:** You need a business email address using your company domain. 

3\. Policy Compliance

Your business must comply with the [WhatsApp Business Terms of Service](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/business-terms) and [Meta's Terms for WhatsApp Business](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/meta-terms-whatsapp-business?lang=id). Certain industries, such as businesses involving alcohol, weapons, or adult services, are prohibited from using WABA. 

4\. Dedicated Phone Number

- You must register a mobile or landline phone number that can receive calls or SMS for the initial verification process.

- This number will be dedicated to WABA and cannot be changed after finalization.

- VoIP, toll-free, or premium-rate numbers are not supported. 

By meeting these requirements, your business can take advantage of WABA's advanced features, such as automation, analytics, and multi-agent capability.
