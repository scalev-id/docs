The 4 places are:

1. Order list

3. Order detail

5. Transaction detail

7. Downloaded file

\===

## 1/ Order list:

Go to the [Orders](https://app.scalev.id/order) menu

Click this icon to filter orders that are already paid.

![Cleanshot 2025 03 17 at 09.43.22](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/CleanShot-2025-03-17-at-09.43.22.png)

Configure like this (check "Paid"):

![Cleanshot 2025 03 17 at 09.41.25](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/CleanShot-2025-03-17-at-09.41.25.png)

Then configure the columns to display (we want to show the Scalev Fee column) by clicking this icon:

![Cleanshot 2025 03 17 at 09.44.59](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/CleanShot-2025-03-17-at-09.44.59.png)

Set like this (check "Scalev Fee" & click "Save"):

![Cleanshot 2025 03 17 at 09.44.05](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/CleanShot-2025-03-17-at-09.44.05.png)

⇣

It will look at least like this:

![Cleanshot 2025 03 17 at 09.47.18](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/CleanShot-2025-03-17-at-09.47.18-1024x411.png)

In the example above, using the [Basic plan](https://scalev.id/plan).

So the Scalev Fee is 1%.

\===

## 2/ Order detail:

When you click one of the orders above, you enter the order detail.

And if you scroll down to this section:

![Cleanshot 2025 03 17 at 09.48.20](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/CleanShot-2025-03-17-at-09.48.20-1024x889.png)

The Scalev Fee amount will appear.
