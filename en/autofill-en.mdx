Scalev has released a new update called **autofill**.

Autofill is a feature that auto-fills forms on Scalev landing pages at checkout.

So a customer who has previously checked out or ordered at one Scalev store, when they order at a different Scalev store, will have their personal data — name, phone, email, etc. — **auto-filled**.

Illustration:

- Agus visits LP A to order a product from your store

- Agus fills in name, phone/email, and address (Physical Product) on the checkout form

- Agus visits your store again later within a 31-day window

- This time Agus visits LP B or another LP in your store that may sell a different product than LP A

- When Agus tries to check out again, the personal data is already auto-filled

- Agus just clicks the submit button once and can check out immediately

Similar to the checkout process at typical marketplaces.

At any store the customer shops at, the personal data is already auto-filled.

As if the customer had already created an account before.

This is one of the frictions that makes people reluctant to shop on websites other than marketplaces.

The hurdle is having to create an account first.

With this autofill feature, Scalev removes that friction.

## How the Autofill Feature Works in Scalev

Here's how it works:

If the form is filled on a **Custom Domain**, it will only work for the same domain, but not including all subdomains of that domain. For example:

- Filling a form on `tokoagus.com`

- When opening any LP on `tokoagus.com`, the form data auto-fills

- When opening `form.tokoagus.com`, the form data does not auto-fill

- When opening `agus.myscalev.com`, the form data does not auto-fill

If the form is filled on a myscalev.com subdomain, it will work for all myscalev.com subdomains but not for Custom Domains. For example:

- Filling a form on `agus.myscalev.com`

- When opening `budi.myscalev.com`, the form data auto-fills

- When opening `tokoagus.com`, the form data does not auto-fill

- When opening `form.tokoagus.com`, the form data does not auto-fill

That's how the autofill feature works in Scalev.

This feature is available to all Scalev users.
