## Disclaimer:

1. Meta verification is "Optional/Not Mandatory"

3. To verify a domain in Meta, you MUST use a Custom Domain — you cannot use Scalev's free Sub-Domain.

5. What domain verification in Meta does, you can read in the Meta article → [https://id-id.facebook.com/business/help/286768115176155?id=199156230960298](https://id-id.facebook.com/business/help/286768115176155?id=199156230960298)

⇣

If you understand, follow the tutorial below:

- Go to your Meta Dashboard.

- Click Brand Safety & Suitability

- Click Domain.

- Then click Add

![Cleanshot 2024 08 07 At 08.28.46](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/CleanShot-2024-08-07-at-08.28.46-1024x580.png)

- Then enter the domain to verify.

- Click Add

![Cleanshot 2024 08 07 At 08.29.50](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/CleanShot-2024-08-07-at-08.29.50.png)

* * *

- Under "Select one option" choose "Update the DNS TXT record with your domain registrar"

- Then copy the TXT Record code

![Cleanshot 2024 08 07 At 08.30.35](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/CleanShot-2024-08-07-at-08.30.35.png)

* * *

- Then go to your DNS settings in Cloudflare.

- Click Add Record.

![Cleanshot 2024 08 07 At 08.56.58](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/CleanShot-2024-08-07-at-08.56.58-1-1024x486.png)

* * *

- Then paste the TXT Record you copied.

- For Name, enter @

![Cleanshot 2024 08 07 At 08.57.49](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/CleanShot-2024-08-07-at-08.57.49-1-edited.png)

* * *

- Once done, go back to the Meta dashboard.

- Click Verify.

![Cleanshot 2024 08 07 At 08.58.59](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/CleanShot-2024-08-07-at-08.58.59.png)

* * *

- If successful, the status will become Verified.

- If it hasn't worked yet, click Verify periodically for up to 72 hours. 🙏

![Cleanshot 2024 08 07 At 08.59.26](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/CleanShot-2024-08-07-at-08.59.26.png)
