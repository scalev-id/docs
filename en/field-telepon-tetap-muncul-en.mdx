If you enable OVO payment method on the Scalev checkout form, the Phone field becomes mandatory and automatically turns ON.

It can't be hidden.

If you feel the Phone field is already disabled but still appears in the landing page form, check the OVO payment method settings — it may be enabled, which is why.

Note that if your OVO payment method is active, payment duration is only 1 minute.

So your store buyer needs to complete payment promptly.

Why only 1 minute?
