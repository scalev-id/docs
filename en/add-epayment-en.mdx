To add an Epayment payment method, follow these steps.

1. In your Scalev dashboard, go to the Stores menu

3. Select your Store name from the list

5. Scroll to the Payment Methods section then click Select Payment Method

A dialog like this will appear.

<figure>

![Tambah Epayment 2](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/tambah-epayment-2.png)

<figcaption>

Adding Epayment to your Scalev store

</figcaption>

- In the red box section, click the dropdown and select Invoice

- Check the blue box "Enable ALL E-Payment Method"

- Click the Save button in the dialog

To make sure your payment method settings are saved, click the Save button in the top right corner of the screen.

At this point you've finished adding the Epayment payment method to your Scalev form.
