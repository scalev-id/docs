As of today, Scalev can directly integrate with courier aggregators "Lincah" & "Mengantar".

So orders in Scalev:

- No longer need to be exported/imported.

- Can request pickup DIRECTLY INSIDE Scalev, for these 2 courier aggregators.

⇣

# What about other courier integrators?

From my side, as the owner of Scalev, I'm happy to integrate with any courier aggregator.

I'm already fine with Scalev's built-in couriers being barely used by Scalev users :D

⇣

It's up to your favorite courier aggregator service to decide whether they want to integrate directly with Scalev.

Because integration is not a one-sided decision :D

⇣

Here's what you can do: **Request** directly to the courier aggregator service.

⇣

The more people request, the more likely that courier aggregator will want to collaborate with Scalev (direct integration).
