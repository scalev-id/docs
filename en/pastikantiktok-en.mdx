To verify that TikTok pixel events fire correctly in Scalev.

Do these 6 things:

## Step 1

Make sure you've connected the TikTok pixel in Settings → Analytics → TikTok Pixel  
_Note: leave the testing code empty unless you're testing_

![Screen Shot 2025 01 09 At 09.39.42](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Screen-Shot-2025-01-09-at-09.39.42-1024x592.png)

## ⇣

## Step 2

Make sure the event trigger is enabled in your store settings.  
[](https://storage.3.basecamp.com/5878785/blobs/6f474fdc-ce35-11ef-a485-1ab7a0b123c9/download/Screen%20Shot%202025-01-09%20at%2009.46.59.png)

![Screen Shot 2025 01 09 At 09.46.59](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Screen-Shot-2025-01-09-at-09.46.59.png)

## ⇣

## Step 3

Make sure the TikTok pixel is set on your landing page.  
[](https://storage.3.basecamp.com/5878785/blobs/8f48f48e-ce35-11ef-9248-0e9c1bc662d2/download/Screen%20Shot%202025-01-09%20at%2009.40.48.png)

![Screen Shot 2025 01 09 At 09.40.48](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Screen-Shot-2025-01-09-at-09.40.48.png)

## ⇣

## Step 4

When a prospective customer opens your landing page, the TikTok pixel in Ads Manager will show the View Content event.

![Screen Shot 2025 01 09 At 09.59.06](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Screen-Shot-2025-01-09-at-09.59.06-1024x351.png)

## ⇣

## Step 5

When a prospective customer fills out the landing page form, the TikTok pixel in Ads Manager will show the Initiated Checkout event.  
[](https://storage.3.basecamp.com/5878785/blobs/0931a69c-ce36-11ef-89f7-8e49e4c60525/download/Screen%20Shot%202025-01-09%20at%2010.00.53.png)

![Screen Shot 2025 01 09 At 10.00.53](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Screen-Shot-2025-01-09-at-10.00.53-1024x392.png)

## ⇣

## Step 6

When you change the payment status from unpaid to paid, the TikTok Complete Payment event won't automatically check — you need to refresh the page first before the checkmark appears.
