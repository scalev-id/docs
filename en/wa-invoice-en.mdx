---
title: "How to Change the WhatsApp Number on the Invoice Page"
description: "The WhatsApp number on the invoice follows 2 WhatsApp numbers:"
---
The WhatsApp number on the invoice follows 2 WhatsApp numbers:

**First:**

The Owner's Business WhatsApp number registered in Scalev, if no Sales Person is assigned to that store 🙏

→ **How to change it:** Go to Setting → Profile → Phone Number for This Business → Edit

![Cleanshot 2025 07 04 at 10.05.56@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-04-at-10.05.56@2x-1024x568.png)

**Second:**

The Sales Person's number assigned to that store.

→ **How to change it**: Log in to Scalev using the Sales Person account → go to Setting → Profile → Phone Number for This Business → Edit

* * *

The WhatsApp number on the invoice page can also be disabled:

Go to Store → Public Order Page → Open

Then uncheck Show WhatsApp Button → Save → Save Store settings
