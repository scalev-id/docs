Text version:

Sometimes you as an Owner who created an LMS product in Scalev want to see your own LMS product from a member's perspective.

Just as a member (student) would see your LMS product in their own member area.

Before this article was published, there was no dedicated Owner login page.

Now there is.

Steps:

Add the slug **/member/owner** to the main domain configured in Store settings.

<figure>

![Pengaturan domain store](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/pengaturan-domain-store-1024x640.png)

<figcaption>

Domain settings in your Store

</figcaption>

</figure>

This becomes a complete link like this.

Example:

https://**yourdomain.com**/member/owner

Access it in your favorite browser.

Once opened it looks roughly like this:

<figure>

![Login as owner](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/login-as-owner-1024x640.png)

<figcaption>

Login as Owner

</figcaption>

</figure>

Enter your Scalev account credentials to log in.

If you successfully log in, the view looks roughly like this:

<figure>

![Dasbor lms owner](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/dasbor-lms-owner-1024x640.png)

<figcaption>

Owner-only LMS dashboard

</figcaption>

</figure>

The Owner-only LMS dashboard shows all the LMS products you've created.
