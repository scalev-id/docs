"_Payment Required_" means: a warning from Scalev because you're unknowingly about to exceed the limit.

Here are the likely things you just did:

Possibility #1: Added a new store.

Free users can only have 1 store.

- Why are you creating a new store? You haven't even maxed out the one you already have.

- And now you want to create another!

- Focus! 😏

Possibility #2: Added a member to your store.

- Why are you adding members right now?

- Make a lot of money first.

Once you're making money, enough to pay someone…

You can totally [upgrade](https://app.scalev.id/setting/billing) your Scalev plan to a paid one! 😏

Possibility #3: Publishing the fourth landing page.

Free users get 3 landing pages only.

- Nice isn't it, building landing pages in Scalev?

- Going fast with the landing pages, huh?

- Need more than 3 landing pages?

- [Upgrade](https://app.scalev.id/setting/billing) your Scalev plan then 😏

Possibility #4: Integrating a tool with Scalev.

If you're already subscribing to tools that automate your time & effort…

You can totally [subscribe to a paid Scalev plan](https://app.scalev.id/setting/billing)! 😏

THE BOTTOM LINE?

The bottom line is you as a FREE user need to upgrade your plan if you don't want the "_Payment Required_" warning to appear.

- Full details on Scalev plans here: [https://scalev.id/plan](https://scalev.id/plan).

- Ready to upgrade? Go to the **"_Billing_**" menu here: [https://app.scalev.id/setting/billing](https://app.scalev.id/setting/billing).
