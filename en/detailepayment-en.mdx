There are 2 fees.

1. Payment

3. Transfer

Let's go through them one by one.

Starting with number 1 (payment).

\===

**Payment:**

![5](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/5-461x1024.jpg)

1. Total amount paid by the customer

3. Fee from Xendit (payment gateway)

5. Tax paid to the government (11% × 4,000)

7. Net amount that enters the Scalev user's balance from that transaction

\===

**Transfer:**

![6](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/6-461x1024.jpg)

- Rp 6,914 = 1% of the amount.

- Rp 6,914 is the Scalev fee.
