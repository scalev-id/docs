#### **Important:**

Before broadcasting, make sure you've set up the payment method and activated your WABA in Meta Business Manager.

For how to activate WABA, see → [Here](https://tutorial.scalev.id/payment-waba/)

If done, follow the steps below:

![Cleanshot 2025 07 18 at 15.23.11@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-18-at-15.23.11@2x.png)

- Go to Ads & WhatsApp → Send WhatsApp Message.

- Select the WABA number you want to use.

- Click Send Message.

![Cleanshot 2025 07 18 at 15.36.29@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-18-at-15.36.29@2x.png)

- Select the Broadcast template you want to use (if you don't have a broadcast template yet, create one in Meta first — learn how [here](https://youtu.be/Wyi55hGD8tw) )

- Upload the Customer data file (your customer phone numbers).

- Click Send

* * *

##### ⚠️ **Notes:**

- Click "Download Template" to create a customer data file in the format Scalev accepts.

- Open the file in Google Sheets for easier editing.

- Enter your customer's name & phone number

- Download as CSV.

![Cleanshot 2025 07 18 at 15.34.49](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/CleanShot-2025-07-18-at-15.34.49.gif)

- If you check "**Prevent Sending to Existing Customer**", customers who have contacted WABA in your Scalev Chat won't receive the Broadcast (excluded from broadcast recipients).

- If you check "**Prevent Sending to Active Customer**", customers who received your broadcast within the last 7 days won't receive your new Broadcast (excluded from broadcast recipients).

Scalev WABA is part of the Waylev (Scalev Chat) service.
