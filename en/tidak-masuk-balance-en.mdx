##### **In short:**

- Bank Transfer Payment Method → Funds go directly to your Personal Bank Account.

- COD Payment Method → Funds go to the Courier Aggregator you use (Lincah/Mengantar)

- **Only E-Payment Payment Method** → Funds go to the Balance menu.
