NOTE Regarding the payment instruction page

- Currently QRIS, VA, and eWallet payment method wording cannot be customized

- All Epayment payment methods: customer is sent directly to the invoice page.

- If order form data is incomplete, the customer is sent directly to the invoice page without going through the payment instruction page.
