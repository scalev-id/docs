title: "The Benefits of the Contacts Feature for Product Owners and Marketers in Affiliate"
description: "So that when there's something to sort out between both parties..."
---
So that when there's something to sort out between both parties...

- Marketers can reach out to product owners...

- Product owners can reach out to their product's marketers...

Neither party needs to contact Scalev's team (cs@scalev.id)...

⇣

Better to handle it directly between yourselves.

So Scalev doesn't have to get involved in your "_internal affairs_" either.

⇣

- A marketer who feels the product owner isn't acting properly can simply stop promoting.

- A product owner who feels a marketer isn't acting properly can remove or ban them from promoting the product.

- Need to clarify something? Need to confirm something? Need to straighten something out? Need to issue a warning? Use the contact access on the card being discussed here.
