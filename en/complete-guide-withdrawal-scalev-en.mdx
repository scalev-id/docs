IMPORTANT Regarding EST Falling on SATURDAY/SUNDAY/MONDAY or Public Holidays & Joint Leave Days

If the EST date shown in the Balance menu falls on a Saturday/Sunday/Monday or on a Public Holiday & Joint Leave Day, the settlement process is usually delayed 1 - 2 days from that EST date.

This matches information from Xendit, which states:

_`Settlement will be processed on the next business day because many banks & clearing systems don't fully operate on weekends.      In addition, inter-bank reconciliation or manual checks (for certain transactions) can add 1–2 business days, and if there's a national holiday near the EST, it can add more delay.`_

So please be patient 🙏

## Withdrawal Tutorial for E-Payment+

If you use e-payment+, the withdrawal method differs from regular e-payment in Scalev.

With regular e-payment, you can withdraw directly from the Scalev dashboard, while with e-payment+ withdrawal cannot be done through Scalev.

To withdraw your e-payment+ balance, log in to the Xendit dashboard.

Login link: [https://dashboard.xendit.co](https://dashboard.xendit.co)

Use your Xendit account login details.

Not your Scalev account.

**Scalev Login ≠ Xendit Login**
