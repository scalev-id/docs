To make a button direct readers/customers to a specific area on your landing page.

Follow these steps:

- Open your landing page.

- Click Add component.

- Select Button.

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/CleanShot-2024-05-08-at-16.00.10.png)

- Then change the button type to Scroll Target.

- Select where you want the Scroll Target to point.

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/CleanShot-2024-05-08-at-16.00.50.png)

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/CleanShot-2024-05-08-at-16.01.29.png)

  

Once done, don't forget to click Save on your landing page.

\---

## Notes

1. If you choose Scroll Target: Back to Top, the reader/customer will be sent directly to the top of your page.

3. If you choose a component, the reader/customer will be sent directly to that component.
