How to Integrate Email Auto Responder (Birdsend) with Scalev

Before integrating Birdsend with your Scalev account, read these terms:

- 1 Birdsend account can only be integrated with 1 Scalev account.

- After integration, try filling out the order form for a product linked to a Birdsend sequence.

- Make sure your Birdsend account is set to single Opt-in (see image).
