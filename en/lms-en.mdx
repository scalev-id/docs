**There are 3 parts to this tutorial:**

- How to assign a custom domain

- How to create a product and enter the LMS Studio menu

- How to add materials

All three must be done before creating an LMS product.

## \[REQUIRED\] How to Assign a Custom Domain

Your LMS product **MUST** assign a domain to the store first.

Whether it's using a domain with Scalev's default subdomain (example.myscalev.com),

or with your own custom domain.

This needs to be done so that your members can access it smoothly.

Also so that the automatic notification email sent when members purchase your LMS product can be delivered smoothly.

**Without a domain = cannot sell LMS products.**

Here's how to assign a custom domain:

![Update domain lms](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/update-domain-lms-1.png)

Select the Stores menu

1/ Open the Store menu then click your store name

![Update domain lms](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/update-domain-lms-2-1024x660.png)

Click the Storefront Settings menu

2/ On the right side there is a widget, then click **Storefront Settings**

![Update domain lms](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/update-domain-lms-3-1024x576.png)

3/ A popup dialog appears, then select your active domain and click the Save button

After completing the domain assignment for your store, you can proceed to create an LMS product.

## How to Add Materials to the LMS

There are 2 levels of material settings in the LMS:

- **Section**, which acts like a folder for modules

- **Module**, the smallest unit of our material

There are 2 types of sections:

- Section **agreement**,

- Section **regular**

There are 2 types of modules:

- Text,

- Video

### **Creating a Section**

![CleanShot 2025 03 13 at 16.22.29@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-03-13-at-16.22.29@2x.png)

Section Types: Regular and Agreement

What's the difference between regular and agreement?

- **Regular** is a **section** type that will contain all your materials. In one LMS there can be many **sections** where each **section** can contain many **modules**.

- **Agreement** is a special **section** type that will open first when your member enters the LMS and is about to start consuming it.
    - Your Customer/Member cannot proceed to a **regular section** before agreeing to all **modules** within the **agreement section**.

## Troubleshooting LMS-related errors

### Error 1

![Error save lms min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/error-save-lms-min.png)

Product Failed to Save

**Problem**: This error appears because your store has not assigned a domain.
**Solution**: Assign the store domain first, [scroll here for how to assign a domain](#how-to-assign-a-custom-domain "scroll here for how to assign a domain").

### Error 2

![Lms wajib domain min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/lms-wajib-domain-min.png)

Add LMS product

**Problem:** This error appears when you want to add an LMS product to your store, but you can't because the domain hasn't been assigned to the store.
**Solution**: Assign the store domain first, [scroll here for how to assign a domain](#how-to-assign-a-custom-domain "scroll here for how to assign a domain").

### Error 3

![Domain cannot be deleted min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/domain-tidak-dapat-dihapus-min.png)

Deleting the store domain

**Problem:** You want to delete a domain that was previously assigned to the current store but when you try to delete it, you can't.
**Solution:** Delete the LMS products that currently exist in your store first, then you can delete the domain.

![SCR 20260102 jbnp](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/SCR-20260102-jbnp-1024x899.png)

Delete LMS product in the store
