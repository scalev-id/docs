## Team Member Role List

Here are the team member roles and the privileges associated with each:

Admindown-arrow1up-arrow

**Admin** can manage everything related to:

- GTM

- Form

- Page

- Member Roles

- Orders

- Store

- Bundle

- Product

- Analytics

- Customer

- Warehouse

- Business Members

- Custom Domain

- Invitation Code

- Payment Account

- Inventory

- Moota Integration

- Business Warehouse Partner

- BirdSend Integration

- Apps Integration

In addition, Admin can also:

- Edit Business

- View Business Transactions

Warehouse Admindown-arrow1up-arrow

**Warehouse Admin** can manage everything related to:

- Inventory Flow.

- Inventory Balance.

⇣   
Warehouse Admin can also manage orders as a Fulfillment Provider including:

- View Order.

- Change Status Order.

- Upload Order Receipt.

- Created AWB.

⇣   
In addition, Warehouse Admin can also view:

- Bundle.

- Product.

- Warehouse.

- Warehouse Partner.

- Payment Account.

Accounting Staffdown-arrow1up-arrow

**Accounting Staff** can manage everything related to:

- Inventory Balance.

⇣ In addition, Accounting Staff can only view:

- GTM.

- Member Roles.

- Order.

- Store & Store List.

- Bundle.

- Facebook & TikTok Pixel.

- Product.

- Warehouse & Warehouse Partner.

- Custom Domain.

- Inventory Flow.

- Payment Account.

- Birdsend Integration.

- Business Transaction.

- Apps Integration.
