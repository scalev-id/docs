Say you already have a Scalev FREE plan account.

You've tried out Scalev's features and now want to upgrade to access a more complete set of features.

- For example, to [add a custom domain](https://tutorial.scalev.id/customdomain/ "OVERVIEW FREE Sub Domain & Custom Domain di Scalev") so you can build your own brand,

- Or to get a lower [Scalev fee](https://tutorial.scalev.id/skemaepayment "fee Scalev"),

- Or maybe to publish more landing pages (the free plan only lets you publish 3),

- Etc.

→ You can start by upgrading from the **Free** plan to the **Lite** plan.

Follow the tutorial below.

* * *

## 5 Plan Types in Scalev

Scalev offers 5 plan types:

- **Free**

- **Lite**

- **Basic**

- **Pro**

- **Ultimate**

Full pricing details at [https://scalev.id/plan](https://scalev.id/plan)

## How to Upgrade from FREE to Lite

1\. Log in to your Scalev dashboard

2\. In the bottom-left corner, click the **Billing** menu > Click the **Upgrade** button.

## How to Upgrade from a Paid Plan to a Higher Plan

For example, you're on Lite and want to move up to Basic, Pro, or Ultimate.

**You don't need to pay the full price.**

Just pay the difference based on the remaining active days of your current plan (prorated).

Example:

If you've been on Lite for 10 days and want to upgrade to Basic, the system calculates the remaining 20 days — so you only pay the **difference between 20 days of Basic and Lite**.

Steps are the same:

1. Go to the **Billing** menu > Click the **Upgrade** button.

3. Select your target plan > Click **Upgrade**.

5. The system will show the amount due.

7. Complete the payment.

## FAQ

**Q: Will my data be lost when I upgrade?**  
A: No, all existing data from your previous plan stays intact.

**Q: Can I downgrade back to FREE?**  
A: Yes, after your paid subscription period ends.

**Q: Can I downgrade to a lower paid plan?**  
A: Yes, using the same process as upgrading — just click the Downgrade button to the desired plan.

**Q: What payment methods are available?**  
A: Bank transfer (Virtual Account), Credit Card, and QRIS (depending on available options at the time). You can also use your **Active Balance** — earnings from product sales in Scalev.
