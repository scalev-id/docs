## **Screenshot Tutorial:**

1. Make sure your Scalev account plan is [Basic](https://app.scalev.id/setting/billing). Adding a CS (sales person) is only possible on Basic plan and above. Plans below that (Lite & Free) cannot.

3. The CS has been [added to your Scalev account](https://app.scalev.id/setting/staff/add).

3\. Add the CS in the "Sales Person" section (in "[Stores](https://app.scalev.id/store)").

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/CleanShot-2025-08-26-at-15.13.42.png)

4\. Set the percentage of incoming orders they receive.

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/CleanShot-2025-08-26-at-15.13.51.png)

5\. Set their work schedule (work days & hours).

![](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/CleanShot-2025-08-26-at-15.14.01.png)
