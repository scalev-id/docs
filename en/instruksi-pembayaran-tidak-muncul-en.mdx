This happens because your order status is Created, meaning the order data received is incomplete.

The question is: what data is actually required? It depends on the product type:

#### **Physical Product:**

![CleanShot 2025 11 28 at 09.22.57@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/CleanShot-2025-11-28-at-09.22.57@2x-1024x982.png)

Required data:

1. Name

3. Phone number

5. Email (optional)

7. Location required (subdistrict & home address)

9. Payment method

11. Courier

If you use COD payment and want the courier to appear, follow: [https://tutorial.scalev.id/pembayaran-cod/](https://tutorial.scalev.id/pembayaran-cod/ "https://tutorial.scalev.id/pembayaran-cod/")

If everything is enabled in the order form but courier still doesn't appear, follow: [https://tutorial.scalev.id/unavailable-courier/](https://tutorial.scalev.id/unavailable-courier/ "https://tutorial.scalev.id/unavailable-courier/")
