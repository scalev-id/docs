title: "How to Create Terms of Service"
description: "A pop-up will then appear."
---
Go to this link: [https://www.shopify.com/tools/policy-generator/terms-and-conditions](https://www.shopify.com/tools/policy-generator/terms-and-conditions)

- Click Create Term and Conditions Policy

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.54.17](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.54.17-1024x586.png)

* * *

1. Enter your Business Name (can be your store name or website name)

3. Enter your email

5. Enter your address.

7. Enter your city.

9. Enter your postal code.

11. Select Country.

13. Select your Province.

15. Enter your website link.

17. Click Send me my term of service policy

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.55.37](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.55.37-1024x589.png)

* * *

A pop-up will then appear.

Then click "Download Now"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.56.46](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.56.46.png)

* * *

- A PDF file will open — copy all of it.

- Then go to [https://app.scalev.id/pages](< https://app.scalev.id/pages>)

- Click Add Landing Page

- Select Blank Page.

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 09.32.24](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-09.32.24.png)

* * *

- Click add component.

- Select text.

- Paste the terms of service you just copied.

- Then adjust as needed.

- Once done, set the Slug & landing page name.

- Then click the "Save & Publish" button

Example: [https://go.elkareem.store/terms-of-service-elkareem](https://cart.scalev.id/terms-of-service-elkareemhttps://cart.scalev.id/terms-of-service-elkareem)
