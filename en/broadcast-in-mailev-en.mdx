#### **10 Steps to Create a Broadcast Email in Mailev**

- **First**, write/paste the email content you want to broadcast.

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 07.16.02@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-07.16.02@2x-1024x671.png)

* * *

- **Second**, upload the logo (optional)

- **Third**, enter the email Footer

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 07.16.52@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-07.16.52@2x-1-1024x796.png)

* * *

- **Fourth**, enter the sender name (free text)

- **Fifth**, enter the sender email

- **Sixth**, select the domain to send the email from.

- **Seventh**, enter the business email you want to use as the reply-to address for replies from your customers.

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 07.17.46@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-07.17.46@2x-1024x582.png)

* * *

- **Eighth**, enter the email subject

- **Ninth**, define recipients (+Add Filter)

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 07.19.00@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-07.19.00@2x-1024x515.png)

**Note: recipients here are your customers in the Scalev customer menu.**

- You can filter customers by product/tag attribute.

- You can target customers who ordered specific products to receive the broadcast.

- You can target customers based on order status to receive the broadcast.

* * *

- **Tenth**, choose whether to send the broadcast Immediately, Scheduled, or Save for later (Draft)

![CleanShot 2026 02 13 at 07.20.52@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CleanShot-2026-02-13-at-07.20.52@2x-1024x540.png)
