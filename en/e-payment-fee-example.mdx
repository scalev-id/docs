There are 2 fees applied to each ePayment transaction:

1. **Payment** — the Payment Gateway Fee charged by Xendit
2. **Transfer** — the Scalev Fee charged upon withdrawal

Each fee is explained in detail below.

---

### Payment Fee

![Payment Fee Detail](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/5-461x1024.jpg)

1. The total amount paid by the customer
2. The fee charged by Xendit (payment gateway)
3. Tax remitted to the government (11% × IDR 4,000)
4. The net amount credited to the Scalev user's balance for that transaction

---

### Transfer Fee

![Transfer Fee Detail](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/6-461x1024.jpg)

<Info>
  **IDR 6,914 = 1% of the transaction amount.**

  This IDR 6,914 represents the Scalev Fee applied at the time of withdrawal.
</Info>
