**Step 1**  
\- Open the Gmail app on your phone.

\- Then click on any email from Scalev — the sender's name is **Army Scalev**.

![Whitelist 1 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-1-min-1024x636.jpeg "Gmail 11")

* * *

**Step 2**  
\- Click the Army Scalev email profile picture (gray photo/Scalev logo).

![Whitelist 2 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-2-min-1024x1024.jpeg "Gmail 11")

\- A pop-up will appear from the bottom. Then click the icon pointed to by the red arrow.

![Whitelist 3 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-3-min-461x1024.jpeg "Gmail 11")

* * *

**Step 3 (last)**  
\- Click the checkmark to save the Army Scalev contact.  
\- If you have more than 1 active Gmail account, first select the one that receives emails from Scalev.

![Whitelist 4 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-4-min-461x1024.jpeg "Gmail 11")

\- Then "_Contact Saved_" will appear.

![Whitelist 5 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-5-min-461x1024.jpeg "Gmail 11")

(✓) Done. "_cs@scalev.id_" has been saved to your Gmail contacts.

# _"What if the_

_Army Scalev email \_\_has already_ _ended up in SPAM,_  
_what do I do?"_ **🙄**  
**(Mobile too)**

**Step 1**  
\- Open the Gmail app on your phone. Then click the three lines in the top-left corner.

![Whitelist 10 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-10-min-1024x666.jpeg)

\- Then click the "_spam_" folder.

![Whitelist 6 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-6-min-461x1024.jpeg "Gmail 11")

* * *

**Step 2**

![Whitelist 7 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-7-min-1024x969.jpeg "Gmail 11")

\- Click the Army Scalev email that ended up in the spam folder.

![Whitelist 8 Min](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/whitelist-8-min-864x1024.jpeg "Gmail 11")

\- Then click "_Report not spam_".

* * *

Note:

\- "_Report not spam_" above only clears that one email that ended up in spam.

\- To make sure future emails don't land in spam, do the **"_whitelist"_** steps described above.

* * *

🔷 After whitelisting, Army Scalev emails will land in your inbox (primary). Not spam. 

🔷 You won't miss any content and you'll minimize having to dig through the spam folder.

🔷 It's **HIGHLY RECOMMENDED** to whitelist when the first Army Scalev email arrives in your inbox.
