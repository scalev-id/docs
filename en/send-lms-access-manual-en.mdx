As a seller, you can resend the LMS access email to your student/customer.

Useful when:

1. A student claims they never got the email. Just resend it. Avoid unnecessary disputes.

3. A student entered the wrong email during purchase. After fixing the email in the order, they need access sent to the correct email — just resend the access.

5. You accidentally disconnected your domain or subdomain from your store. If a sale happened during that time, you can now resend the access.

7. Other situations where you need to resend LMS access to the buyer's email.

**Tutorial:**

1. Go to the order.

3. Find the "Send LMS Access" button.

5. Click it

# By the way…

Why is it called "**RESEND**"?

Because normally access is automatically sent to the buyer/student's email once payment is confirmed.

- Either because the payment method [uses epayment](https://tutorial.scalev.id/tambah-epayment/)

- Or because the [payment method uses bank transfer with unique number & Moota integration (so payment against the invoice is auto-detected)](https://tutorial.scalev.id/integrasimoota/).

So the "Send LMS Access" feature is a safety net.

For the 4 unusual cases explained above.
