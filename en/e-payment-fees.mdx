This article covers the **Scalev Fee** — the fee charged by Scalev on orders that are paid using ePayment methods.

The following payment methods are classified as ePayment:

- QRIS
- VA (Virtual Account)
- GoPay
- LinkAja
- DANA
- OVO
- ShopeePay

<Info>
  **Bank transfer payments at Scalev are completely free — no fees are charged whatsoever.**
</Info>

### Closing Note 1 — Transaction Fee vs. Scalev Fee

The fee discussed in the video above refers specifically to the **Scalev Fee**.

There is a separate fee charged by the payment gateway provider, known as the **Transaction Fee**.

You can view the full Transaction Fee details here: [https://scalev.id/transaction-fee](https://scalev.id/transaction-fee)

<Note>
  Every platform — without exception — charges a transaction fee to its users. This is an industry-wide standard, and Scalev is no different.
</Note>
