If accessing your domain shows a warning like this:

![Image](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/image.png)

![CleanShot 2026 04 15 at 15.27.39@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/CleanShot-2026-04-15-at-15.27.39@2x-1024x717.png)

![Image](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/image-2-1024x560.png)

Your domain has most likely been blocked by Komdigi.

Check your domain at [https://trustpositif.komdigi.go.id/welcome](https://trustpositif.komdigi.go.id/welcome)

Click Cari Data Pemblokiran Trustpositif

![CleanShot 2026 04 15 at 15.37.45@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/CleanShot-2026-04-15-at-15.37.45@2x-1024x611.png)

Enter your domain & click Cari

![CleanShot 2026 04 15 at 15.39.16@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/CleanShot-2026-04-15-at-15.39.16@2x-1024x620.png)

If the status is "**Ada**" (Exists), your domain is indeed blocked by Komdigi.

![CleanShot 2026 04 15 at 15.36.48@2x](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/CleanShot-2026-04-15-at-15.36.48@2x-1024x603.png)

## Solution:

1. \[Recommended\] Use a new domain! _Before buying, make sure the domain isn't blocked by Komdigi._ or...

3. Submit a Normalization Request — click Pengajuan Normalisasi & follow all the instructions.
