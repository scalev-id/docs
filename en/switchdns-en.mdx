## Disclaimer!

Before performing _Switch to New Configuration_ Custom Domain, make sure these 2 things:

1. Make sure your DNS manager supports DNS type CNAME — if not, use Cloudflare. Tutorial → [https://tutorial.scalev.id/cloudflare/](https://tutorial.scalev.id/cloudflare/)

3. Turn off your ads first, because during the custom domain migration your domain won't be accessible.
