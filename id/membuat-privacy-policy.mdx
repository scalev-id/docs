title: "Tutorial Membuat Privacy Policy"
description: "Tutorial Membuat Privacy Policy"
---
Silahkan masuk ke link: [https://www.privacypolicygenerator.org/](https://www.privacypolicygenerator.org/)

Kemudian...

1. Centang bagian "Website"

3. Klik "Continue & Generate Privacy Policy"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.45.18](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.45.18-1024x587.png)

* * *

- Masukan link URL Website

- Masukan Nama Website

- Untuk Entity Type:

1. Pilih "I'am a bisnis", Jika Bisnis Anda mengguna data badan usaha.

3. Pilih "I'am an Individual", Jika Bisnis Anda mengguna data pribadi.

- Pilih negara Indonesia

- Klik "Next Step"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.46.42](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.46.42-1024x585.png)

* * *

- Centang data apa saja yang Anda hidupkan pada form pemesanan

- Klik "Next Step"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.47.31](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.47.31-1024x592.png)

* * *

- Centang By visiting a page our website

- Masukan Link Landing page Anda

- Klik "Next Step"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.48.52](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.48.52-1024x587.png)

* * *

- Centang pada bagian "No, I don't want a Professional Privacy Policy"

- Klik "Next Step"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.49.46](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.49.46-1024x850.png)

* * *

- Masukan email Anda.

- Lalu klik "Generate"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 09.29.46](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-09.29.46.png)

* * *

- Scroll Ke Bawah.

- Lalu klik " Copy this to clipboard"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.52.34](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.52.34-1024x723.png)

* * *

- Pergi ke https://app.scalev.id/pages

- Klik Tambah landing page

- Pilih Blank Page.

- Tambahkan komponen & pilih html.

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 09.33.31](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-09.33.31.png)

* * *

- Paste HTML Code yang tadi Anda copy.

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 09.36.52](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-09.36.52.png)

- Atur slug & nama landing pagenya.

- Kemudian klik "Simpan & Terbitkan"

Contoh: [https://go.elkareem/privacy-policy-elkareem](https://cart.scalev.id/privacy-policy-elkareem)
