---
title: "Tutorial Membuat Terms of Service"
description: "Nanti akan muncul pop up."
---
Silahkan menuju link: [https://www.shopify.com/tools/policy-generator/terms-and-conditions](https://www.shopify.com/tools/policy-generator/terms-and-conditions)

- Klik Create Term and Conditions Policy

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.54.17](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.54.17-1024x586.png)

* * *

1. Masukan Nama Bisnis Anda (Bisa nama toko, bisa nama website)

3. Masukan email Anda

5. Masukan alamat Anda.

7. Masukan Kota Anda.

9. Masukan kode pos Anda.

11. Pilih Negara.

13. Pilih Provinsi Anda.

15. Masukan link website Anda.

17. Klik Send me my term of service policy

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.55.37](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.55.37-1024x589.png)

* * *

Nanti akan muncul pop up.

Lalu klik "Download Now"

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 07.56.46](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-07.56.46.png)

* * *

- Nanti akan terbuka file PDF, Silahkan Anda copy semuanya.

- Lalu pergi ke [https://app.scalev.id/pages](< https://app.scalev.id/pages>)

- Klik Tambah Landing Page

- Pilih Blank Page.

![Cleanshot 2025 02 06 At 09.32.24](https://tutorial.scalev.id/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CleanShot-2025-02-06-at-09.32.24.png)

* * *

- Klik tambah komponen.

- Pilih text.

- Paste terms of service yang tadi sudah Anda copy.

- Lalu sesuaikan.

- Jika sudah silahkan atur Slug & Nama landingpagenya.

- Kemudian klik tombol "Simpan & Terbitkan"

Contoh: [https://go.elkareem.store/terms-of-service-elkareem](https://cart.scalev.id/terms-of-service-elkareemhttps://cart.scalev.id/terms-of-service-elkareem)
